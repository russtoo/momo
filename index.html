About Us

We are Cafe Momo Restaurant and Coffee Shop, a home-grown brand born out of a passion for quality food and crafts work. Our story began by providing quality food and excellent service to offices and homes, as well as outdoor events. We then expanded our business to include mobile food units, which allowed us to serve fast food on a daily basis. This was the main driver of our growth and success which led to launching Cafe Momo, a Coffee Shop and casual Restaurant.

Cafe Momo has a simple design which leans towards an artistic feel. We used rustic elements to create an artistic texture and a warm atmosphere. Our aim is for our customers to enjoy not only the food, but the ambiance as well. We offer Western foods though focusing on health-oriented foods. We do not deep fry our food, rather we flame grill it!

In addition, we are committed to empowering youth and creating employment opportunities. We source our ingredients from local farms and producers to ensure quality and sustainability.

We are proud of our journey and we look forward to serving you with our mouth-watering dishes and services.
